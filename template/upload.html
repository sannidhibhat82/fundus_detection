This project focuses on the automated detection of fundus diseases using deep learning techniques. With the increasing prevalence of eye-related diseases such as diabetic retinopathy, glaucoma, and cataract, early and accurate diagnosis is crucial for effective treatment. Our solution leverages advanced image processing and machine learning algorithms to analyze retinal images and provide reliable predictions.

The system aims to assist healthcare professionals by reducing the time and effort required for manual evaluation while increasing diagnostic accuracy and consistency.

Problem Statement

  • Time-Consuming Process: Manual assessment by ophthalmologists takes considerable time.
  • High Error Rate: Human evaluations are prone to errors, leading to misdiagnoses.
  • Inconsistent Results: Variability in assessments can lead to inconsistent diagnostic outcomes.
  • Accessibility Issues: Limited availability of specialists makes timely diagnosis difficult in many regions.

Objectives

  • Conduct a comprehensive analysis of existing research on fundus disease detection.
  • Acquire and preprocess a dataset relevant to the problem statement.
  • Develop a robust deep learning model for detecting conditions such as diabetes, glaucoma, and cataract.
  • Evaluate and compare the results of the developed model with those of existing models.
